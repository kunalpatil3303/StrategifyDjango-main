Strategify

Strategify is a web platform working towards providing it's users tools to test their trading strategies to get a better understanding of how well their plans may work in the future based on analysis of historical data. Our easy-to-use website makes it easy for even the laymen to test their plans and make improvisations to them as and when needed.

Our Vision

At Strategify, we understand the importance of our client's money. A well tested trading plan is the foundation for survival and profits in the Markets. If you are thinking of trading in the Stock Market, a well tested trading strategy can make all the difference. We wish to encourage the people's participation in the Stock Markets by providing a platform for testing their strategies risk free.

Our Features

You can create and test your strategies using on any number of years.
